Dog Food

Your four-legged friend rewards you in many ways. Whether it’s accompanying you on a brisk jog to help you stay in shape, or giving you a cuddle when you need it most, there’s no denying how rewarding a pup can be. Show your appreciation and reward your furry friend with the foods and dog treats they crave.

Chewy carries a wide range of delicious dog foods and tasty dog treats for your precious pup. When it comes to choosing a dog food, you may find all the options overwhelming. Let us help you find the best one with dog food reviews, ingredient information, and personal one-on-one attention when you give us a call at 1-800-672-4399. At Chewy we understand the important role dog food plays. Most of the nutrients your dog needs to stay strong and healthy comes from the food they eat. Chewy carries healthy dog food brands like Blue Buffalo dog food, Royal Canin dog food, Taste of the Wild dog food and Hill’s Prescription Diet dog food. We even carry grain-free dog food, limited ingredient dog food for pups with food allergies or sensitivities and diet dog food.

Dry dog food is great for the pup parent who is away all day. Dry dog kibble can be left out for your canine companion to snack on throughout the day without spoiling. Dry dog kibble can also be good for your dog’s teeth -crunchy kibble helps pups get rid of pesky plaque and tartar. Wet dog food often contains flavors and ingredients canines love. Freeze-dried dog food is another option, the limited ingredients in freeze-dried dog food varieties makes it a great dog food for sensitive stomachs or food allergies. And you may also consider feeding your pup dehydrated dog food. Dehydrated dog food is created by taking the whole foods found in a raw doggie diet, and slowly heating them until they’re dehydrated. An added bonus: Dehydrated dog food is easy to transport for pup parents who are on the go. And, frozen dog food is another great option. These doggie meals offer your pet the nutrition he was meant to consume in the wild and are rich in protein and typically contain less additives. Whether it’s dry kibble your dog desires or a grain-free wet food your canine craves, you can find this and more at Chewy's online pet store where you find the best dog supplies.
